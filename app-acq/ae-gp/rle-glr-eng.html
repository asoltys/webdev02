Green Language Repertoire

PDF Version (B)
Help on File Formats

The Green Language Repertoire is a compilation of exact wording taken from existing Solicitation documents (Requests for Standing Offers, Requests for Supply Arrangements and Requests for Proposals).

Procurement personnel can use this Repertoire to assist them in developing evaluation criteria, clauses and conditions, statements of work or supplier surveys that must include environmental considerations.

This Repertoire will be updated periodically by the Green Procurement Team in the Acquisitions Branch of PWGSC.

The Green Procurement Team can be contacted at: Achatsecologiques.greenprocurement@tpsgc-pwgsc.gc.ca

Please note: The sample language was gathered from existing solicitation documents that have been previously issued on Merx. However, please do not assume that this language has been previously approved by Policy or Legal Services. To use the language in upcoming solicitation documents, procurement personnel must:

  1. Tailor the wording to fit the commodity in question;
  2. Ensure that all wording is approved by Policy or Legal Services.

Please stay tuned: Acquisitions Branch is working to develop standard clauses and conditions related to green procurement.
